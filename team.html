Our Team

We have a team of qualified dental nurses who are able to make your visit to the practice as comfortable as possible. We also support the mentoring of young trainee nurses to gain experience shadowing our more experienced team members.

Our dedicated receptionist will be a very familiar face to most patients, having worked at the practice for 27 years! She has a great rapport with all the existing patients and helps to put people at ease when they first arrive.
